Historic Legends & Lore

A dramatic clifftop fortress perched on a rocky headland in northeastern Scotland. Dunnottar was the home of the Earls Marischal and hid the Scottish Crown Jewels from Cromwell's invading army.

Essential Travel Intel

Parking: Available (Steep path to castle entrance)
Best Photo Spot: Clifftop pathway from the north
Admission: Ticketed (£10.50 adult)
